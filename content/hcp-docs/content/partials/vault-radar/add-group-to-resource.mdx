Once you add a data source, an HCP user with the `admin` role must assign
a group to each of the monitored resources within each data source. You can
assign each resource to only one group.

<Note>

Users with the HCP IAM admin role do not need to be a member of other groups. Accounts
with the admin role have full access to Vault Radar.

</Note>

If you do not already have a group, refer to the [Identity and Access Management
groups documentation](/hcp/docs/hcp/iam/groups) to create a group.

1. Navigate to the **Project dashboard**.

1. Click **Access control (IAM)**.

1. Click **Add new assignment**.

1. Search the name of the group in the **Search for an assignee** search field.

1. Click the group name in the search results.

1. Click the **Select service** pulldown menu and select **Vault Radar**. 

1. Click the **Select role** pulldown menu and select the **Vault Radar
   Developer** role.

1. Click **Save**.

1. Click **Back to Dashboard**.

1. Click **Vault Radar**.

1. Click **Resources**.

1. Select the resource you want to assign to a group and click **Assign groups**.

1. Click the **Assign resoruce to group** pulldown menu.

1. Select the group that requires access to the resource.

1. Select either the **Viewer** or **Contributor** role.

1. Click **OK**.
