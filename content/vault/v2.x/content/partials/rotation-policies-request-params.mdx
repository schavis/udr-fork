- `max_retries_per_cycle` `(int:<required>)` - The maximum number of times Vault Enterprise
  should apply backoff for rotation entries after a rotation failure.

- `max_retry_cycles` `(int:<required>)` - The maximum number of times Vault
  Enterprise should requeue rotation entries after reaching the
  `max_retries_per_cycle` limit. Vault Enterprise requeues rotation entries to their
  next scheduled rotation.
