### Sidebar menu reloads on engine-scoped pages ((#sidebar-menu-reloads-on-engine-scoped-pages))

| Change      | Status | Vault edition | Affected version | Fixed version |
| ----------- | ------ | ------------- | ---------------- | ------------- |
| Known issue | Open   | All           | 2.0.0            | No            |

#### Issue

On some engine-scoped GUI routes, the Vault sidebar can briefly render the wrong
menu or reload during navigation before settling on the correct section.

The menu flicker is most noticeable on subpages like the **Custom messages**
page under **Operational tools**, and secret-detail routes. The flicker occurs
during page transitions and does not affect the underlying operation.

#### Recommendation

Continue with the affected workflow after the page finishes loading
and the sidebar settles into the correct state.
